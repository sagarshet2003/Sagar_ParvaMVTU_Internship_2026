Welcome to RajaRajeswari College of Engineering, where quality education, innovation, and excellence come together to shape successful future engineers.

Introduction

RajaRajeswari College of Engineering, established in 2006, is a premier institution committed to excellence in technical education. Located on the Kumbalagodu, the college offers a dynamic learning environment with modern infrastructure, diverse engineering programs, and a strong focus on innovation, research, and overall student development.

Vision

To empower young minds through technology, research, and innovation to create skilled and socially responsible professionals.

Mission

To provide holistic, innovative education through technology, research, and skill development, shaping ethical, skilled professionals ready to meet global challenges.

About the College

Year of Establishment : 2006

RajaRajeswari College of Engineering (RRCE) in Bengaluru was established in 2006 by the Moogambigai Charitable and Educational Trust under the leadership of Dr. A. C. Shanmugam. Affiliated with VTU, Belagavi and approved by AICTE, the institution quickly grew from its inception to become a recognized technical center offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs.

Founder / Principal's Message: Our goal is to nurture students into responsible and successful individuals.
