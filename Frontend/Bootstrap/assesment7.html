Toyota Motor Corporation is a premier Japanese multinational automotive manufacturer, founded in 1937 by Kiichiro Toyoda and headquartered in Toyota City, Aichi. As the world’s largest automobile manufacturer, it is renowned for its "Quality, Durability, and Reliability" (QDR) and the pioneering "Toyota Production System" (TPS), focusing on just-in-time production and waste elimination.

Toyota Kirloskar Motor Pvt. Ltd (TKM), established in 1997, is a leading Indian automaker and a joint venture between Japan's Toyota Motor Corporation (89%) and the Kirloskar Group (11%).
